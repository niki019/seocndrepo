# MedConnect 🏥

**AI-Powered Telemedicine Platform for Global Healthcare Consultations**

MedConnect is a healthcare platform designed to help doctors manage international patients through a single end-to-end solution. The platform streamlines patient onboarding, voice-based symptom collection, doctor matching, appointment scheduling, secure video consultations, prescriptions, follow-up tracking, and patient engagement.

## 📌 Problem Statement

Doctors consulting international patients often rely on multiple disconnected tools for appointments, video consultations, prescriptions, payments, and follow-ups. This fragmented workflow results in:

- Lost patient context
- Poor follow-up compliance
- Increased administrative effort
- Inefficient patient management

MedConnect solves this by bringing the entire patient journey into one integrated platform.

---

## 💡 Solution

MedConnect enables patients to:

- Describe health concerns using voice input
- Receive AI-assisted doctor specialization recommendations
- Schedule consultations with qualified doctors
- Make secure online payments
- Attend in-app video consultations
- Access prescriptions and consultation reports
- Complete periodic health check-ins

Doctors can:

- Manage patients through a unified dashboard
- Conduct video consultations
- Generate reports and prescriptions
- Monitor patient progress
- Track follow-up compliance
